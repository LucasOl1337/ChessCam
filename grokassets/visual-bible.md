# Visual Bible — ChessCam

**Core Metaphor**: Chess + Camera / Computer Vision. A tool that watches a real chessboard (or screen) and brings deep analysis, AR overlays, or streaming capabilities. The meeting point between physical chess, digital vision, and powerful analysis.

**Emotional Promise**: "I set up my camera over the board and suddenly I have a grandmaster analyzing every move with me in real time."

**Visual DNA Keywords**: Chessboard as sacred grid, camera eye / lens as active observer, AR overlays on physical pieces, clean analysis panels, tension between analog wood and digital vision.

**Color System**:
- Classic chess (deep green/black + cream/white) + modern vision tech accents (electric blue or teal for the "camera" layer)
- High contrast for board readability

**Typography Direction**: Classic chess elegance meets modern tech clarity.

**Audience & Cultural Notes**: Chess players (serious amateurs to pros) who want technology to enhance (not replace) the beauty of the game. Streamers, coaches, students.

**Forbidden Styles**:
- Pure cartoon chess
- Overly sci-fi without respecting the game

## Reference Assets

Logo: Beautiful lens or camera aperture framing a chess position, with analysis lines elegantly overlaid.

Hero: A real wooden chessboard with a camera above it, digital AR analysis glowing on the pieces, player in deep focus.
